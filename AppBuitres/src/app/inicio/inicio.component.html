AQUA CONTAINER

About Aqua container proyect


The main objective of our project is to raise the existing problems in the supply of drinking water to the domestic sector in the ZMG, from 2010 to 2016, where significant changes were made in the sources of supply, as well as in the way of organizing for meet the demand for that resource in the metropolitan area. With this information collected, it will be possible to identify the areas with the highest rate of affectation, which will be useful for the investment of resources that can help to solve the problem presented.






The Metropolitan Area of Guadalajara (AMG) is integrated with the municipalities of:


  • • San Pedro Tlaquepaque
  • • Tonalá
  • • Zapopan
  • • Tlajomulco de Zúñiga
  • • El Salto
  • • Juanacatlán
  • • Ixtlahuacán de los Membrillos
  • • Guadalajara
